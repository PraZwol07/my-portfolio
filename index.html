Process

How an engagement usually runs.

Not a rigid framework — but most projects move through the same three shapes.

Understand the real constraint

Before any design work, I spend time with the actual data, users, or hardware involved. Most interesting constraints aren't in the brief.

Prototype in the real medium

I skip static mockups where I can and prototype directly in code, so what you're reviewing behaves like the real thing.

Ship, then simplify

First versions tend to do too much. I build in a pass, after launch, dedicated purely to removing what didn't earn its place.
